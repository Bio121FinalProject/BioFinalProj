Pesticide Use

  • Like the cultivation of many other crops, the usage of pesticides can be harmful to local wildlife.
  • The use of rodenticides can cause severe tissue damage that often kills or severely disables to animal that consumes them, according to a 2018 study.
  • Runoff into water streams can contaminate drinking water
  • Solid quality can be severely depleted
  • Nearby plants can be harmed
    • Which can result in lower crop yield for some crops other than marijuana
